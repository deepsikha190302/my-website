Finding the journey


by

A venturer




Life is a journey, long and winding
Full of twists and turns, with no clear finding
We wander through it, seeking our way
Hoping to discover what we should do each day
Sometimes we stumble, and fall to the ground
Our hearts heavy, and our spirits unsound
But we mustn't give up, we must persevere
For the path to our destiny will soon become clear
Our journey is unique, no two are the same
And the road we travel is not for fame or gain
It's to find our purpose, and to fulfill our soul
To make a difference, and to reach our goal
So let's not be afraid to take the first step
To embrace the journey, with all its depth
For in the end, when we look back and see
Our journey was worth it, and we were meant to be.
